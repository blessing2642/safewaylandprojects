About

Safeway Land Projects core business is the provision of housing infrastructure and fencing solutions.

Vision

To see an equitable urban society where every low income urban citizen is decently accommodated.

Mission

To deliver decent and affordable residential accommodation to every low income citizen throughout Zimbabwe

7 Years of Experience

Safeway Land Projects is a Zimbabwean registered and domiciled entity operating in the housing development services arena, and its core business is the provision of housing infrastructure.

Our Services

Land Development
